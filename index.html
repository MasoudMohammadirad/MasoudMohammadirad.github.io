Linguist

My research is centered around synchronic studdy of Iranian languages and language documentation in the Kurdish region. My work touches many areas, including language contact, morphology, semantics, word order, and corpus linguistics.

My Background

About me

I am a Postdoctoral researcher at Cambridge University with a Ph.D. in linguistics from Sorbonne Nouvelle, Paris III. Currently, I am working on the doucmentation of Hawrami, one of the most conservative Iranian languages.
